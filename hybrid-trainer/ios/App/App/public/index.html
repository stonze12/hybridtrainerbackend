Your Goal

Choose your primary goal. This adjusts your calorie target above or below maintenance and shifts your macro ratios accordingly.

🔥
Cut — Lose Fat
Maintain muscle while reducing body fat. ~300–500 kcal deficit. High protein to protect lean mass during training.
Best for: fighters cutting to a weight class
⚖️
Maintain — Fighter Performance
Eat at maintenance. Optimised for training output, recovery, and performance — the Ch.13 default for the 5-day program.
Best for: fighters at competition weight
💪
Lean Build — Gain Muscle
Modest ~200–300 kcal surplus with high protein. Slow, controlled muscle gain without excessive fat. Aggressive bulk not recommended for fighters.
Best for: off-season muscle building
🔄
Recomposition — Build & Burn
Eat near maintenance with very high protein. Lose fat and gain muscle simultaneously — slower than dedicated cut/bulk but no sacrifice phase needed. Works best for beginners or those returning after a break.
Best for: newer fighters, returning from injury
